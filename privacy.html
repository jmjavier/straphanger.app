Privacy Policy

StraphangerApp — Last updated April 2, 2026

What we collect

StraphangerApp requests access to your device's location to identify the nearest subway station. Location data is used only on-device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

What we don't collect

We do not collect personal information, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or any data that leaves your device.

Third-party services

StraphangerApp fetches real-time train arrival data from the MTA public API. No personal information is sent as part of these requests.
